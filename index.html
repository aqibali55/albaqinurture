About Al Baqi

Transforming medical education through innovative learning

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality medical education resources that empower healthcare professionals to excel in their careers.

Al Baqi was founded in 2015 by a group of medical educators who recognized the need for more accessible, high-quality exam preparation resources for medical professionals in our region. What began as a small question bank has grown into a comprehensive learning platform serving thousands of doctors across multiple specialties.

Our Approach

We combine evidence-based learning science with practical clinical knowledge to create the most effective preparation tools. Our team of expert physicians and educators continuously update our content to reflect the latest exam patterns and medical knowledge.

Unlike traditional resources, our platform adapts to each learner's strengths and weaknesses, providing personalized recommendations to maximize study efficiency.
